Picture Perfect

From artistic interiors to dramatic cliffs, Rizal is a massive playground for photographers and content creators.

Pililla Wind Farm

Gigantic wind turbines set against rolling green hills, offering a perfect backdrop for cinematic sunset shots.

Angono Street Murals

Walk down Doña Aurora Street where walls are transformed into concrete masterpieces of local folklore.

Daranak Falls

Use a slow shutter speed to capture the silky, majestic flow of this highly photographed 14-meter cascade.

Pinto Art Museum

Every corner of this white-walled, Santorini-inspired museum is carefully designed for aesthetic portrait photography.

Regina RICA

Capture the awe-inspiring scale of the 71-foot statue of the Virgin Mary towering over the canopy of trees.

Tinipak River

A landscape photographer's dream featuring jagged white limestone rocks contrasting against turquoise waters.
