Sustainable Adventures

Support vital conservation efforts by visiting Rizal's protected areas and community-led eco-parks.

Masungi Georeserve

An award-winning conservation area known for its suspended "Sapot" web and trails designed for minimal environmental impact.

Mt. Purro Nature Reserve

An eco-park dedicated to reforestation where visitors can hike, plant trees, and support the local indigenous community.

Pacem Eco Park

A quiet sanctuary focused on environmental education, featuring organic gardens, butterfly sanctuaries, and wildlife rescue.

Tinipak River Conservation

A community-managed tourism site where local fees directly support the preservation of the river's pristine marble waters.

Ynares Eco System (YES)

Visit various municipal parks transformed by the provincial government's massive greening and cleanliness campaign.

Wawa Eco Park

A protected gorge where local boatmen and guides lead sustainable tours, protecting the historical dam from degradation.
