Authentic Rizal

Go beyond the tourist spots and immerse yourself in the daily life, traditions, and local crafts of the Rizaleños.

Pottery Making

Get your hands dirty and learn the ancient art of molding clay at the Crescent Moon Cafe and Studio.

Bamboo Weaving

Visit local communities in Tanay and learn how the famous "sawali" walls and intricate baskets are crafted by hand.

Kakanin Cooking

Watch local vendors in Cainta expertly mix, steam, and layer the colorful ingredients of their famous rice cakes.

Angono Art Walk

Join a guided walking tour through the backstreets of the Art Capital, visiting independent studios and local painters.

Dumagat Tribe Visit

Trek into the Sierra Madre to interact with the indigenous Dumagat people, learning their sustainable way of life.

Laguna de Bay Fishing

Experience a peaceful morning in Cardona or Binangonan by joining local fishermen as they harvest from the lake.
