Peaks of the Sierra Madre

Escape to the highlands of Tanay and Rodriguez for breathtaking sea-of-clouds views, limestone formations, and fresh mountain air.

Mt. Daraitan

A favorite for hikers seeking a sea of clouds at the summit and a refreshing dip in the marble-walled Tinipak River below.

Mt. Pamitinan

Offers thrilling rock scrambling over limestone towers and a panoramic view of the Wawa Dam and the Sierra Madre range.

Treasure Mountain

Accessible and beginner-friendly, famous for its majestic sea of clouds and rock formations without the need for a strenuous trek.

Mt. Binacayan

The twin mountain to Pamitinan, known for its steep, jagged limestone trails and spectacular sunrise views over Rodriguez.

Mt. Hapunang Banoi

The highest of the Montalban trilogy, offering a challenging climb rewarded with sweeping views of lush, untouched valleys.

Mt. Sipit Ulang

Named after crab claws, this trail features unique cave-like rock formations and a fun, highly technical scramble to the peak.
