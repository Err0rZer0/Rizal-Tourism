Escape to Serenity

Rejuvenate your senses with Rizal's luxury mountain resorts and vast nature parks designed for ultimate relaxation.

Loreland Farm Resort

A vast, multi-pool complex in Antipolo perfect for family staycations, team buildings, and grand summer getaways.

Luljetta's Hanging Gardens

The country's first hanging gardens resort, offering breathtaking infinity pools overlooking Laguna de Bay.

Punta de Fabian

An elegant hilltop resort in Baras offering luxurious accommodations and a spectacular view of the lake.

Thunderbird Resorts

A world-class resort in Binangonan featuring golf courses, fine dining, and casino entertainment.

Momarco Forest Cove

Hidden in Tanay, this resort blends nature and comfort, featuring a massive pool and an artificial waterfall.

Bakasyunan Resort

A sprawling 15-hectare property packed with activities like ATV rides, mud karts, and multiple swimming pools.
