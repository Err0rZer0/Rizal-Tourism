About Rizal Tourism

Discover the gateway to the Sierra Madre and the artistic soul of the Philippines.

Our Province

Located just east of Metro Manila, Rizal Province is a unique blend of urban development and pristine natural beauty. Named after the national hero, Jose Rizal, our province serves as the artistic capital and the primary destination for nature enthusiasts in the region.

Our Mission

To promote sustainable tourism that preserves our mountains, rivers, and cultural heritage while providing unforgettable experiences for every traveler.
