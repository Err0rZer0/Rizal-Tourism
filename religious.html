A Pilgrimage of Faith

Rizal is the heart of religious devotion, home to century-old stone churches and the International Shrine of Our Lady of Peace and Good Voyage.

Antipolo Cathedral

The home of the Black Madonna, attracting millions of pilgrims every May for the traditional "Alay Lakad."

Regina RICA

A 13.5-hectare sanctuary in Tanay featuring a towering 71-foot statue of the Virgin Mary, ideal for silent reflection.

Boso-Boso Church

A 16th-century stone church built by Jesuits, standing as a beautiful, rustic testament to the history of the province.

St. Jerome Parish

Located in Morong, this 17th-century church features a striking neo-baroque facade and a dramatic octagonal bell tower.

Our Lady of Light Parish

A historic church in Cainta featuring a beautiful stone facade and paintings by National Artist Fernando Amorsolo.

St. Joseph Parish

One of the oldest churches in Rizal, located in Baras, featuring a distinct dark adobe stone structure and a checkerboard floor.
