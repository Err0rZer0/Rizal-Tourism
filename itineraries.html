Curated Rizal Itineraries

Whether you have one day or an entire weekend, these simple paths will lead you to the best of Rizal.

The Nature Lover's Day Trip (Tanay)

Active
  • 05:00 AM: Sunrise at Treasure Mountain (Sea of Clouds)
  • 08:00 AM: Breakfast at a local Tanay carinderia
  • 10:00 AM: Swimming at Daranak Falls
  • 01:00 PM: Lunch with a view at Wyndhaven or Escalera
  • 03:00 PM: Quick visit to Regina RICA

The Arts & Culture Circuit (Angono & Antipolo)

Cultural
  • 09:00 AM: Explore the Angono Petroglyphs
  • 11:00 AM: Blanco Family Museum or Pinto Art Museum
  • 01:00 PM: Lunch at Burrow Café or Crescent Moon Cafe
  • 03:00 PM: Visit Antipolo Cathedral (Our Lady of Peace and Good Voyage)
  • 05:00 PM: Sunset viewing at Cloud 9
