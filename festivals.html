Vibrant Traditions

Celebrate the colorful and rhythmic soul of Rizal through its world-famous street festivals and religious traditions.

Higantes Festival

Angono's grand feast featuring giant paper-mâché caricatures parading the streets every November in honor of San Clemente.

Sumilang Festival

Antipolo's celebration of bountiful harvests, specifically highlighting the local suman, mangga, and kasoy.

Hane Festival

Tanay's founding anniversary festival celebrating eco-tourism, local culture, and agricultural abundance.

Sikaran Festival

Baras' unique festival showcasing the ancient Filipino martial art of Sikaran, which focuses almost entirely on kicking.

Hampang Kalabaw

A fascinating tradition where local farmers train their water buffaloes to kneel as a sign of respect during the town fiesta.

Alay Lakad

A massive penitential walk every Maundy Thursday and May 1 where millions of devotees hike up to the Antipolo Cathedral.
