Bring Home a Piece of Rizal

From woven crafts to sweet delicacies, take the best of Rizal's local industries and homegrown treats back home.

Taytay Tiangge

The "Garments Capital of the Philippines," offering high-quality and affordable clothing for wholesale and retail.

Antipolo Pasalubong Center

Located just outside the Cathedral, this is the ultimate spot for buying bundles of suman and freshly roasted kasoy.

Crescent Moon Pottery

Buy beautifully handcrafted stoneware and ceramic plates straight from the artisans of Antipolo.

Cainta Kakanin Stalls

Line up to buy the province's best sapin-sapin, kalamay, and bibingka from local heritage vendors.

Angono Art Souvenirs

Take home miniature Higantes dolls, local paintings, and woven crafts from the Art Capital's dedicated souvenir shops.

Tanay Public Market

Find fresh mountain produce, wild honey, and indigenous woven bamboo products straight from the local farmers.
