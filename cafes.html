Coffee with a View

Experience the "Tagaytay of the East" with overlooking cafes that offer the best brews, hearty meals, and views of the Metro Manila skyline.

Burrow Café

An underground hidden sanctuary in Antipolo offering a rustic, forest-view dining experience with incredible breakfast meals.

Wyndell's

A popular spot in Tanay where you can enjoy hot chocolate and local meals while watching the sunset over the mountains.

Cloud 9

Famous for its hanging bridge and 360-degree view of the city lights and Laguna de Bay from its expansive viewing deck.

Kaulayaw Coffee

An open-air cafe championing locally sourced Philippine coffee beans, paired with a stunning overlooking view of the sunset.

ArtSector Gallery & Cafe

A stunning, multi-level architectural masterpiece in Binangonan offering contemporary aesthetics and breathtaking skyline views.

Escalera Cafe

Known for its glass floors and Bali-inspired aesthetic, offering one of the most Instagrammable coffee experiences in the highlands.
